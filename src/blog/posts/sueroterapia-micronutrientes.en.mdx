Intravenous sueroterapia consists of administering vitamins, minerals, antioxidants and other therapeutic molecules directly into the bloodstream, bypassing the digestive system. The result is 100 % bioavailability — compared to a maximum of 20–40 % via the oral route — with faster, more intense and longer-lasting effects.

What two decades ago was exclusive to intensive hospital settings has today become a tool for preventive medicine, anti-ageing and oncology support with growing clinical evidence. At Clínica Les Lilas, in San Juan de Alicante, each IV sueroterapia protocol is designed on an individual basis following medical assessment, with certified pharmaceutical-grade substances and under professional supervision throughout the session.

## What is intravenous sueroterapia?

Intravenous sueroterapia, also known as IV therapy or micronutrient infusion, is a medical technique that administers active substances — vitamins, minerals, antioxidants, coenzymes, amino acids and longevity molecules — directly into the bloodstream through a peripheral venous line.

Unlike a conventional hospital drip, whose function is to hydrate or stabilise, therapeutic sueroterapia uses specific formulations designed to produce concrete biological effects: cellular energy, antioxidant protection, immune support, DNA repair, liver detoxification, neuroprotection or anti-ageing action.

Each infusion is prepared on an individual basis and administered in a controlled clinical environment, with patient monitoring throughout the session.

## Why the IV route and not oral supplements?

It's the question new patients ask most. The answer lies in bioavailability: the proportion of a substance that actually reaches the cells in active form.

The difference is especially relevant for substances such as vitamin C, where high therapeutic doses (25–50 g) are impossible orally without causing digestive discomfort, or glutathione, which is almost completely degraded in the digestive tract before absorption.

In oncology patients, intestinal absorption is often compromised by the effects of chemotherapy itself, making the IV route the only effective way to guarantee real therapeutic levels.

## The main substances and their effects

These are the substances used in Clínica Les Lilas protocols, with their main indications:

**Glutathione IV (600–1200 mg)**
The most powerful antioxidant produced by the body. Its levels decrease with stress, age and disease. In IV infusion: deep liver detoxification, reduction of cellular oxidative stress, skin radiance, oncology support.

**NAD+ IV (125 mg)**
The longevity molecule. Its levels drop by up to 50 % between 40 and 60 years of age. In IV infusion: DNA repair, mitochondrial energy, mental clarity, high-impact anti-ageing protocol. One of the most advanced treatments currently available.

**Vitamin C IV (7.5 g – 50 g)**
At low doses: immune and antioxidant support. At high doses (≥25 g): selective pro-oxidant action on tumour cells, enhancement of the immune system in oncology. The oral route cannot achieve equivalent therapeutic levels.

**CoQ10 IV (150 mg)**
Essential coenzyme for mitochondrial energy production. Indicated for deep fatigue, cardioprotection (especially in patients on anthracyclines), sports recovery and neuroprotection.

**Curcumin IV (150 mg)**
The active principle of turmeric, with oral bioavailability of less than 2 %. In infusion: precision anti-inflammatory, cellular neuroprotection, immuno-oncology support. 100 times more effective than the oral capsule.

**MSM IV (15 %)**
Methylsulphonylmethane. Potent natural anti-inflammatory, especially indicated for chronic joint pain, osteoarthritis, fibromyalgia and peripheral neuropathy.

**Selenium IV (900 mcg)**
Essential trace element for thyroid function, immunity and antioxidant defence. Most of the population does not reach optimal levels. Especially relevant in chronic fatigue and during oncology treatments.

**B Vitamin Complex / Methylcobalamin B12**
Neurological and haematological support. Essential cofactors of energy metabolism. Indicated for fatigue, neuropathy and support during chemotherapy.

**EGCG IV — Epigallocatechin Gallate**
The most active catechin in green tea, with immunomodulatory and pro-apoptotic activity documented in oncology studies. Available from April 2026.

## Who is it indicated for?

IV sueroterapia has two main patient profiles at Clínica Les Lilas:

**Oncology patients**
During or between chemotherapy or radiotherapy cycles: immune support, reduction of side effects, neurological protection, liver detoxification. Protocols are always designed in coordination with the responsible oncologist.

**Wellness and prevention patients**
Active people seeking to optimise their energy, strengthen their defences, slow cellular ageing or treat chronic conditions such as fatigue, joint pain or oxidative stress. You don't need to be ill to benefit from sueroterapia.

## What a session is like at Clínica Les Lilas

**Prior assessment.** Every first session is preceded by a medical evaluation that may include a blood test according to the indicated protocol. This allows doses to be adapted, possible contraindications to be detected and real deficits to be identified.

**The session.** The patient settles comfortably in a chair or on an examination table. A small-calibre peripheral venous line is placed in the forearm. The infusion lasts between 40 and 130 minutes depending on the protocol. During the session, the patient can read, work or simply rest. A healthcare professional is present or available at all times.

**After the session.** No recovery is required. Most patients resume normal activity immediately. Effects can be noticed from the first session, although higher-impact protocols require several sessions for an optimal and lasting result.

## Sueroterapia and oncology: the bridge between the two

Intravenous sueroterapia is a natural bridge between wellness medicine and oncology support. Several of the substances used in wellness protocols have direct applications in supporting patients undergoing oncology treatment:

- **High-dose Vitamin C** acts as a selective pro-oxidant on tumour cells at doses ≥25 g IV, while at lower doses it functions as a general immunomodulator.
- **Glutathione** protects the liver from chemotherapy damage and reduces systemic oxidative stress.
- **Curcumin IV** has anti-inflammatory and immunomodulatory activity with documented interest in an oncology context.
- **EGCG** shows pro-apoptotic activity in in vitro and preliminary clinical studies.

All protocols in an oncology context are carried out in coordination with the patient's oncologist and never as an alternative to conventional treatment.

## Safety and medical framework

IV sueroterapia is a medical act, not an aesthetic procedure. At Clínica Les Lilas, the same rigour is applied as for any clinical intervention:

- Prior medical assessment for every first session
- Blood test according to protocol indication
- Certified pharmaceutical-grade substances
- Individualised preparation of each infusion
- Monitoring during the session by a healthcare professional
- Detection and management of possible drug interactions

Contraindications are few but exist — for example, certain kidney diseases or specific conditions — and are identified during the prior assessment. For this reason, at Clínica Les Lilas no protocol is administered without initial medical evaluation.
