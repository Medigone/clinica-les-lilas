## What is sueroterapia?

Sueroterapia, also known as IV therapy (intravenous therapy), is a medical technique that involves administering micronutrients — vitamins, minerals, amino acids and antioxidants — directly into the bloodstream through an intravenous line.

This route of administration bypasses the digestive system, ensuring nearly 100% bioavailability of the nutrients, compared to only 20-40% through oral intake. It is particularly indicated when nutritional needs are high or digestive absorption is compromised.

## Main indications

Sueroterapia addresses a wide range of clinical situations:

- **Chronic fatigue and exhaustion**: replenishment of B vitamins, magnesium and vitamin C to restore energy levels.
- **Immune support**: strengthening natural defenses through high-dose vitamin C and zinc cocktails.
- **Oncology support**: nutritional support for patients undergoing chemotherapy or radiotherapy to mitigate side effects.
- **Convalescence and recovery**: after surgery, illness or prolonged infection.
- **Oxidative stress and aging**: delivery of powerful antioxidants (glutathione, alpha-lipoic acid) to combat cellular damage.

## Common protocols

Each protocol is individualized following a comprehensive medical assessment. Among the most common formulations:

- **High-dose vitamin C protocol**: from 15g to 75g depending on the indication, used for oncological and immune support.
- **Myers' cocktail**: classic combination of magnesium, calcium, B vitamins and vitamin C, indicated for fatigue and deficiencies.
- **Glutathione protocol**: the body's master antioxidant, administered IV for maximum efficacy.
- **Remineralization protocol**: magnesium, zinc, selenium and trace elements to correct mineral deficits.
